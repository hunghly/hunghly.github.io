Jesus Saves!

Here's the Hope you've been looking for! The Gift of Eternal Life!

1. Realize that you are a sinner.

Romans 3:23 — "For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God."

2. Realize that you need to be forgiven of your sins.

Romans 6:23 — "For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."

3. Realize that Jesus loves you and wants to forgive you.

Romans 10:13 — "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved."

Dear Jesus, have mercy unto me a sinner. Come into my heart and forgive me of my sins. I claim You as my Savior and I place my trust solely on You. Amen.


Just saying this prayer alone won't save you, but putting your faith in Jesus and believing will.

Romans 10:10 - "For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation."
